Ages, Birthdays, and Profiles of Each Member

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 2, 2024

Popular South Korean (K-Pop) boy group Stray Kids currently has eight members, namely Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han Jisung, Felix Lee, Seungmin, and I.N.

Bang Chan is the oldest member of Stray Kids at 26 years old, followed by Lee Know, who is 25 years old. Changbin is 24 years old, Hyunjin is 23 years old while Han Jisung is just about a month younger but also 23 years old. Felix Lee is also 23 years old but is a day younger than Han, while Seungmin is also 23 years old. The current youngest member of the group is I.N., who is just 22 years old.

Stray Kids Members’ Birthdays and Profile

Bang Chan

  • Birthday: October 3, 1997
  • Age: 26 Years Old
  • Role: Leader, lead vocalist, dancer, rapper

Bang Chan was born on October 3, 1997, in Seoul, South Korea, and is currently 26 years old. He is the oldest member and leader of the popular K-pop boy group Stray Kids under JYP Entertainment. As the leader, Bang Chan plays a crucial role in guiding and organizing the group’s activities. He is responsible for setting the overall direction and vision and ensuring that the members work cohesively as a team.

Aside from his leadership role, Bang Chan also showcases his versatility as a lead vocalist, dancer, and rapper. His vocal skills are prominently displayed in the group’s songs, where he delivers smooth and emotive performances. As a dancer, he exhibits precision and charisma, captivating audiences with his powerful stage presence. Additionally, Bang Chan’s rap skills are evident in his participation in the pre-debut sub-unit 3RACHA, where he showcased his lyrical prowess alongside Changbin and Jisung.

Beyond his musical abilities, Bang Chan is multilingual, being fluent in English, Korean, and Japanese. He also has some knowledge of Chinese. This linguistic proficiency allows him to connect with fans from various countries and cultures. Moreover, Bang Chan’s dedication to his craft is exemplified by his seven years of training at JYP Entertainment, honing his talents and preparing for his debut.

Within the group’s dynamic, Bang Chan also takes on the responsibility of cooking for his fellow members. This nurturing role highlights his caring nature and the bond he shares with his groupmates. He is also a proud owner of a King Charles Spaniel dog named Berry, demonstrating his affectionate and gentle side.

Lee Know

  • Birthday: October 25, 1998
  • Age: 25 Years Old
  • Role: Main dancer, sub-vocalist, sub rapper

Lee Know, whose real name is Lee Min Ho, was born on October 25, 1998, making him 25 years old as of today. Lee Know plays a vital role as the main dancer, sub-vocalist, and sub-rapper.

Known for his exceptional dancing skills, Lee Know has been recognized as the main dancer of Stray Kids. His precise and powerful movements captivate audiences, adding a dynamic element to the group’s performances. His impressive control and versatility bring a unique flair to their choreography.

In addition to his dancing prowess, Lee Know also contributes as a sub-vocalist and sub-rapper. His vocal range complements the group’s harmonies, providing a smooth and melodic touch to their songs. As a sub-rapper, he showcases his rap skills, adding a distinct flavor to their tracks.

Before debuting with Stray Kids, Lee Know honed his talents by participating in dance competitions and working as a backup dancer for BTS. This experience undoubtedly contributed to his exceptional dancing abilities. Furthermore, Lee Know has released solo songs and choreographed impressive dance performances, highlighting his creativity and artistry.

Beyond his role as a performer, Lee Know has also taken on the responsibility of being a fixed MC for Show! Music Core. This demonstrates his versatility and ability to engage with audiences in various capacities.

Changbin

  • Birthday: August 11, 1999
  • Age: 24 Years Old
  • Role: Main rapper, sub vocalist

Seo Chang-bin, known by his stage name Changbin, was born on August 11, 1999, a fact that indicates he is currently 24 years old. He is the main rapper and producer of the group, and his role is essential and multifaceted.

As the main rapper, he showcases his exceptional skills in delivering powerful and dynamic rap verses, adding a unique flavor to Stray Kids’ music. His ability to maintain a strong flow and control his tone captivates listeners and contributes to the group’s distinct sound.

In addition to being the main rapper, Changbin also takes on the role of a sub-vocalist. Although his primary focus is on rapping, he displays his versatility by lending his vocal abilities to the group’s harmonies and melodies. This further enhances the group’s performances and allows for a wider range of musical expression.

Changbin’s contributions to Stray Kids go beyond his vocal abilities. He is also involved in producing and creating the group’s music. With his skills as a producer, he actively participates in the writing and composing of songs, showcasing his creativity and musical prowess. This involvement ensures that his unique artistic vision is reflected in the group’s music, allowing for a more authentic and personal connection with their fans.

Changbin’s dedication and passion for his craft are evident in his hard work and determination. He is known for his relentless pursuit of perfection, constantly striving to improve and push the boundaries of his abilities. His commitment to his craft is reflected in the high-quality performances he delivers on stage, captivating audiences with his energy and charisma.

Beyond his musical talents, Changbin is recognized for his caring nature and support for his fellow members. He is known to be a pillar of strength within the group, offering guidance and encouragement when needed. His presence creates a positive and supportive environment, fostering unity and teamwork among the members.

Hyunjin

  • Birthday: March 20, 2000
  • Age: 23 Years Old
  • Role: Main dancer, lead rapper, visual

Hyunjin, born on March 20, 2000, is currently 23 years old. He is a prominent member of the South Korean boy group. As the main dancer, lead rapper, and visual of the group, Hyunjin plays a crucial role in their overall performance and image.

His skills as a main dancer are highly regarded within the group. Hyunjin’s precision, flexibility, and charisma on stage make him a standout performer. His ability to synchronize his movements with the other members showcases his dedication to the craft. Hyunjin’s dance skills contribute to the group’s dynamic choreography, adding an extra layer of energy and synchronization to their performances.

In addition to his dancing prowess, Hyunjin also takes on the role of a lead rapper in Stray Kids. His rap style is characterized by a steady flow, clear enunciation, and a confident delivery that captivates audiences. His verses often bring a unique flavor to the group’s songs, adding depth and variety to their overall sound.

Hyunjin’s visually appealing features further enhance his presence in the group. His striking looks, including his sharp jawline, expressive eyes, and flawless complexion, make him a captivating presence on stage and in music videos. His visuals contribute to the group’s overall aesthetics and help attract fans from all over the world.

Beyond his individual roles, Hyunjin’s contributions to Stray Kids extend to his interactions with his fellow members. He is known for his helpful nature, particularly in assisting I.N. His support and guidance contribute to the group’s unity and camaraderie, fostering a positive and collaborative environment.

Outside of his group activities, Hyunjin has a variety of hobbies and interests. He enjoys dancing, reading books, and playing sports. His passion for dance is evident in his performances, while his love for literature and athletics showcases his multifaceted nature.

Han Jisung

  • Birthday: September 14, 2000
  • Age: 23 Years Old
  • Role: Main rapper, lead vocalist

Han Jisung, also known as Han, was born on September 14, 2000, which invariably makes him 23 years old. Han plays a crucial role as the main dancer and lead vocalist of Stray Kids.

Han brings his exceptional dance skills and energy to the group’s performances. His precise movements and synchronization with the other members contribute to the group’s dynamic and captivating stage presence. Han’s versatility as a dancer allows him to adapt to various dance genres, showcasing his adaptability and talent.

In addition to his dancing prowess, Han also shines as a lead vocalist. His unique and soulful voice adds depth and emotion to the group’s songs. Han’s vocal range and control enable him to deliver powerful, heartfelt performances, captivating listeners with his expressive singing.

Beyond his roles as a dancer and vocalist, Han is also involved in the production of songs for Stray Kids. His contribution to the group’s music showcases his creativity and musicality. Han’s involvement in the production process allows him to play a part in shaping the group’s sound and artistic direction.

Felix Lee

  • Birthday: September 15, 2000
  • Age: 23 Years Old
  • Role: Lead rapper, lead dancer

Felix Lee, known as Lee Felix, was born on September 15, 2000, in Sydney, Australia. He is the only Stray Kid member born outside South Korea, and he is currently 23 years old. Felix holds the significant positions of lead rapper and lead dancer in Stray Kids. His exceptional talent in both rapping and dancing adds a distinctive flavor to the group’s performances.

As a lead rapper, Felix showcases his impeccable flow, lyrical prowess, and powerful delivery, captivating audiences with his unique style. His verses are characterized by their smoothness, precision, and ability to convey emotions effectively. Felix’s rap skills not only enhance the group’s songs but also contribute to their performances’ overall energy and dynamism.

In addition to his rap abilities, Felix also serves as the lead dancer of Stray Kids, showcasing his exceptional skills and versatility in various choreographies. His precise and sharp movements and his natural charisma and stage presence contribute to the group’s captivating performances. Felix’s dancing adds an extra layer of visual appeal to their music videos and live stages, elevating the overall quality of their performances.

Beyond his roles as a lead rapper and lead dancer, Felix is known for his multi-talented nature. He is a skilled beatboxer and often incorporates his beatboxing skills into the group’s live performances, adding an extra layer of creativity and excitement. Felix’s versatility as an artist allows him to contribute to the group’s music production and songwriting process.

Felix’s presence within Stray Kids extends beyond his performance abilities. He is known for his warm and friendly personality, which helps establish a strong bond with fans. His infectious energy and natural charisma make him a valuable asset to the group’s overall dynamics and chemistry.

Seungmin

  • Birthday: September 22, 2000
  • Age: 23 Years Old
  • Role: Main Vocalist

Seungmin was born on September 22, 2000, in Seoul, South Korea. Currently, he is 23 years old. Since joining JYP Entertainment in 2017 after placing second in the 13th Open Audition, Seungmin has been a lead vocalist in Stray Kids. His role as a main vocalist showcases his exceptional vocal range and skills, contributing to the group’s harmonies and powerful performances.

Seungmin’s distinct vocal abilities make him an integral part of Stray Kids’ sound. His smooth and emotive voice adds depth and emotion to their songs, captivating listeners with his passionate delivery. Seungmin’s vocal range allows him to effortlessly handle both high and low notes, demonstrating versatility in various musical genres.

As a main vocalist, Seungmin takes on the responsibility of delivering impactful and heartfelt performances. His performances are characterized by powerful vocal belting, delicate control, and expressive interpretation of lyrics. Seungmin’s ability to convey emotions through his voice allows him to connect with the audience on a profound level, evoking empathy and leaving a lasting impression.

Seungmin’s dedication to his craft is evident through his consistent training and commitment to improvement. His unwavering work ethic is reflected in his practice of writing training logs daily, highlighting his determination to excel in his role as a main vocalist. He continuously hones his skills, always striving for growth and pushing the boundaries of his vocal abilities.

Beyond his vocal prowess, Seungmin brings a unique charm to the group. Fans affectionately nickname him Su, drawn to his chubby left cheek and his warm and friendly personality. His favorite season is autumn, and he is known for being a morning person and the cleanest member of the group. Seungmin’s hobbies include writing in his diary, listening to music, and enjoying good food.

Seungmin’s role models, DAY6, Kim Dong-ryul, and Sandeul from B1A4, inspire him to explore and experiment with different musical styles and concepts. He aspires to delve into darker concepts in the future, showcasing his artistic growth and versatility as a vocalist.

In addition to his musical endeavors, Seungmin has showcased his talents as an MC for ASC (After School Club) and is a fixed guest on KBS BTOB Kiss the Radio. These opportunities allow him to showcase his charisma and communication skills, further solidifying his presence within the K-pop industry.

I.N.

  • Birthday: February 8, 2001
  • Age: 22 years old
  • Role: Sub-vocalist, Maknae

I.N., whose real name is Yang Jeong-in, was born on February 8, 2001, in Busan, South Korea, which makes him currently 22  years old. He is also the make, which means the youngest member of Stray Kids. As the group’s youngest member, I.N. brings a youthful energy and charm to the group.

In terms of his role in the group, I.N. is a sub-vocalist. As a sub-vocalist, he showcases his vocal abilities and contributes to the group’s harmonies and melodies. Despite his young age, I.N. has proven himself as a talented singer with a unique and soothing voice. His vocals often add a refreshing and melodic touch to Stray Kids’ music.

In addition to his vocal skills, I.N. is known for his cheerful and playful personality, which helps create a lively and energetic atmosphere within the group. He brings a sense of innocence and youthfulness to the dynamic of Stray Kids, making him a beloved member among fans.

As the maknae, I.N. is often taken care of and doted on by the older members of the group, who act as his mentors and protectors. This dynamic adds an endearing element to Stray Kids’ interactions and showcases the strong bond between the members.

FAQS

How Old Is Felix Stray Kids?

Felix Stray Kids, real name Felix Lee or Lee Felix, is 23 years old. Felix was born in Sydney, Australia, on September 15, 2000.

When Did Woojin Leave Stray Kids?

Woojin left Stray Kids in 2019, just about one year after their debut in March 2018. He was one of the original members of the group and cited personal reasons as the reason for his departure.

When Will Stray Kids Disband?

Stray Kids may disband in 2025 as their contract with JYP Entertainment is slated to end that year. Whether they decide to renew the contract or explore new paths individually remains to be seen.
