Bae Suzy And Yang Se-jong Are Set To Feature In The Forthcoming Netflix Series Doona

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

On December 12, Netflix officially announced Bae Suzy and Yang Se-jong as the stars in the forthcoming romantic series Doona!, an adaptation of the well-known webtoon The Girl Downstairs by artist Min Song-ah. Lee Jung-hyo, the director of Crash Landing on You, will direct the series. The filmmaker has previously worked on blockbuster shows such as Life on Mars and Romance is a Bonus Book. Doona! follows the blossoming relationship between a retired K-pop star and an ordinary university student. Unavoidable circumstances will force the couple to share a home. Bae Suzy will play retired K-pop star Lee Doo-na, while Yang Se-jong will play Won Joon, an average university student.

The persona of Bae Suzy Lee Doo-na used to be the lead singer of an idol group.

Bae Suzy will play Lee Doo-na, a well-known and important K-pop star. Despite being the primary singer of her group, Doo-na is forced to announce her retirement and notify everyone that she will no longer be connected with her group due to unanticipated circumstances. Following her departure from the K-pop group, the former idol moves into a shared residence near a university. Yang Se-jong will play the male protagonist, Won Joon, who is a typical college student who chooses to share his home with Doo-na.

As Doo-na and Yang Se-jong begin living together, the latter first avoids her, but ultimately gets intrigued by her strange existence and attempts to find out why she has chosen to give up all of her popularity.

In summary, Bae Suzy and Yang Se-jong

Who exactly is Bae Suzy?

Suzy, often known as the “Nation’s First Love,” is one of South Korea’s most recognizable celebrities. She has become one of the most talked-about Hallyu performers in the nation because of her singing and acting abilities. She has been in several blockbuster dramas, including While You Were Sleeping, Vagabond, Gu Family Book, Uncontrollably Fond, Start-Up, and many more. She recently appeared in the thriller series Anna, for which she won critical acclaim. She has previously worked with several other actors, including Lee Jong-suk, Kim Woo-bin, Nam Joo-hyuk, Kim Seon-ho, and others.

Who is Yang Se-jong?

Yang Se-jong, who was released from the military on November 15, 2021, is finally making his K-drama debut alongside Bae Suzy in Doona! The temperature of Love, Thirty But Seventeen, My Country: The New Age, and Duel are among the plays in which he has appeared. He also made a guest appearance in Season 2 of Dr. Romantic in 2020. He has worked with a variety of actresses, including Shin Hye-sun, Kim Seol-Hyun, Seo Hyun-jin, and many more.
