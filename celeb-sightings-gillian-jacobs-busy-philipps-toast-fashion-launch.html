Celeb Sightings: Gillian Jacobs, Busy Philipps Toast Fashion Launch

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, September 20, 2024

—Gillian Jacobs, Busy Philipps, Irene Neuwirth, and Jenny Slate toasted Jordana Warmflash of Novis and her Fall/Winter 2015 ready-to-wear collection at the Irene Neuwirth boutique in West Hollywood, Calif.

—Beyoncé wore Victoria, Victoria Beckham x Nails inc polish in Judo red at the TIDAL launch in NYC.

—Maria Menounos imitated wrestling great Dusty Rhodes while hosting the Hall of Fame ceremony at Wrestlemania 31 at Levi Stadium in San Francisco, Calif.

—SYTYCD’s Stephen “tWitch” Boss sparked a dance off at the Timberland x American Rag party for Timberland's MarkMakers at Edge Studios in L.A.

—Tammin Sursok wore a CC SKYE ring while browsing her cell phone in L.A.

—Kathy Wakile had dinner with a friend at Fresco da Franco in Montclair, N.J.

—Nikki Reed listened to music through her Urbanears Kransen earbuds while walking through LAX Airport.

—Janelle Monáe enjoyed tunes by DJ Lo Down Loretta Brown (aka Erykah Badu) at the Black Girls Rock post-reception presented by Hennessy V.S in Newark, N.J. 

—Naomi Watts and Liev Schreiber took sons Sasha and Kai – both clad in PLAE shoes – to the Djanai's Angels Special Needs Family Prom in L.A.

—January Jones drank a Heineken and caught up with Jessica Pare’s boyfriend John Kastner at AMC's Black & Red Ball celebrating the final season of Mad Men in L.A.

—Extra host Charissa Thompson got her eyebrows waxed at Spa Sophia in L.A.’s Venice neighborhood.

—Nina Agdal celebrated her 23rd birthday with a cake and some tennis at the Lacoste Suite during the Miami Open in Miami, FL.

—Gwen Stefani took her children for a fun lunch at The Cheesecake Factory in L.A.’s Sherman Oaks neighborhood.

—Hilary Duff hung out with her Younger costars before the show’s premiere at Hotel Hugo's rooftop lounge Bar Hugo in NYC.

—Christina Hendricks helped unveil the new 2016 Jaguar XF and 2016 Model Year Range Rover to crowds at Center548 during the New York International Auto Show in NYC.

—Greer Grammer wore Carolinna Espinosa and BLACKSEA at the iHeartRadio Music Awards at the Shrine Auditorium in L.A.

–Foo Fighters’ Pat Smear and actor William Fichtner checked out some iconic shots of Kurt Cobain and Slash by rock ’n roll photographer Kevin Mazur at the Americana at Brand in Glendale, Calif.

—The Daily Show’s Aasif Mandvi spoke about his career at the LinkedIn Discussion Series in NYC.

—Serena Williams warmed up for the Miami Open at the Sixth Annual All Star Charity Tennis Event at The Ritz-Carlton, Key Biscayne in Miami, FL.
