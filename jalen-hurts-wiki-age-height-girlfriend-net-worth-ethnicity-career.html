Jalen Hurts Biography

Jalen Hurts was born in 1998 at the home of Averion Hurts, a football coach at Channelview High School, and Pamela Hurts in Houston, Texas, USA. He is currently in his twenties. Furthermore, Jalen has two siblings: a sister called Kennedy Michelle and a brother named Averion Hurts Jr. His citizenship is American.

Hurts attended Channelview High School in Channelview, Texas. Hurts received a second-team all-district selection as a sophomore and the District 21-6A Overall MVP as a junior during his high school career. During his high school career, he competed against the Texas A&M freshman outstanding running back. Hurts was a four-star recruit who was regarded as one of the top dual-threat quarterbacks in the Class of 2016. Although Texas A&M made a strong push to recruit Hurts after the loss of their starting quarterback, they ultimately failed when Hurts committed to the University of Alabama on June 5, 2015. Alabama’s Defensive Line Coach Bo Davis and Offensive Coordinator Lane Kiffin both recruited him.
