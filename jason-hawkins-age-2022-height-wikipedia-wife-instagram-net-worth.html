Who Is Radio Host Jason Hawkins? Wiki, Biography

Jason Hawkins is a popular radio host and a journalist. Jason ‘Jase’ Hawkins got his start in radio at Triple M, when he volunteered to answer phones in exchange for free Coke cans. Jase relocated to Sydney in 2004, where he created a name for himself in radio by working on the top-rated Kyle and Jackie O Show and anchoring the Hot 30 Countdown, earning him the moniker ‘Labrat.’

He returned to Brisbane a few years later to co-host B105 FM’s Breakfast program, and after seven years, Jase crossed the divide from Australia to New Zealand in 2015, where he met Polly ‘PJ’ Harding and co-hosted National Drive at ZM. Jase and PJ formed an arranged marriage, going from awkward acquaintances to awkward, non-biological brother and sister, earning them a nomination for Best Non-Breakfast show at the NZ Radio Awards two years in a row.

Each weekday morning from 6 am, Jase & Lauren can be heard on the KIIS Network, with the best sections from each day’s show replayed at 8 pm.
