Jazmine Lucero - Bio, Age, Height, In Relation, Nationality, Body Measurement, Career

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, July 7, 2024

Jazmine Lucero is a popular social media personality, singer, actress, and model, from the United States of America. Jazmine Lucero has performed at many shows as part of the band ‘Why Don’t We’. Moreover, she is also passionate about dance and has won a few dance championships.

Early Life

Jazmine Lucero first opened her eyes on 16th August 1992. She is from Long Beach, California, USA, and holds an American nationality. She is 28 years old and Leo is her birth sign. Jasmine is of Hispanic descent. Her mother, Monica has lupus, an autoimmune disease.

Her father played football for ‘Montana State University.’ She has three brothers, namely, Justice, Jaden, and Jacob, and a sister named Paulina. She and popular “Viner” Jordan Dowodzenka are good friends. Similarly, long before she became a popular “Viner”, she and actor Wilmer Valderrama were good friends.

More about herself

She has obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD). Her favorite color is pink. Jasmine loves watching the series ‘Teen Wolf.’ The 1985 American adventure–comedy film ‘The Goonies’ is her all-time favorite movie. Moreover, she is highly adventurous and loves to travel. She is a foodie and loves almost all types of cuisines, especially Italian food.

Rise to Stardom

Jazmine is a multi-talented person. She loved to sing and dance since her childhood. She later developed an interest in modeling, too. Further, she also won the ‘Miss Teen Southern California’ pageant. Simultaneously, she pursued her career in dance. She participated in several dance championships and won some of them, too.

Moreover, she worked as a dance trainer and an in-house choreographer at ‘Kimberley’s Dance Studio’ in Paramount, California. She displayed her singing skills through the bands ‘MoZaiK’ and ‘JCity’ but could not establish herself as a solo artist.

Jasmine still continued to perform in groups and later joined the band ‘Why Don’t We’. She has delivered several live stage performances with the band and has even been on a tour in 2017. She launched her self-titled ‘YouTube’ channel in April 2012 but posted her first video in September 2014.

The channel primarily hosts her personal vlogs, hauls, challenges, pranks, and Q&A videos. A number of the videos on the channel feature her mother and brother. She also generated significant followings through short-form content on Vine and TikTok.

More about her career

She has a collaborative YouTube channel with her former boyfriend, Nick Pallauf. The channel is popular for its weekly vlogs, Q&A videos, pranks, storytimes, and challenges. They broke up in November 2017 and posted a video titled ‘Our Relationship Status,’ through which they shared the reason behind their break-up.

She and Nick continue to upload videos on the channel even after their break-up, and the videos often include the caption “with my ex!”. Moreover, she and Nick are both popular “musers”. Apart from their respective TikTok accounts, they have a collaborative account, too.

They both love to travel, which is well displayed by the name of their clothing line, ‘JAUNT’. Moreover, she also posts her performances on her individual TikTok account ‘@jazminejlucero’ and has over two million fans on the platform. She was also popular on ‘Vine’ before the application was taken down.

She has an individual Instagram page where she posts selfies and other photographs. Moreover, she has a collaborative ‘Instagram’ page with Nick, too, which has over 29 thousand followers. She has been offered a few acting projects, too.

She made her acting debut with a brief appearance in an episode of the 2012 TV series ‘Dream – A Tweeny Musical’. She appeared as ‘Carla’ in the 2013 action short ‘Unshakable’. The same year, she appeared in a brief role in the short film ‘Arcade Fire: Afterlife’. Further, she attended Katia Nicole’s ‘Rave Music Video’ release party on May 4, 2013.
