Jenni &quot;JWoww&quot; Farley, Roger Mathews Set to Wed This October

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Fall it is! Jenni “JWoww” Farley and her fiancé Roger Mathews have finally set a wedding date — the couple reveal exclusively to Us Weekly that they’re tying the knot in New Jersey on October 18.

"I didn’t have a vision going into this I just knew that I wanted to marry Roger and am so excited to finally have a date set,” the former Jersey Shore alum tells Us. "I am so excited to be planning the wedding of my dreams,” Farley, who is planning her big day with Robynne Adoff of Party Extravaganza, added.

As for the guest list, the MTV star tells Us that most of the Jersey Shore cast will be invited while her BFFs Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi and Sammi “Sweetheart” Giancola will serve as bridesmaids.
