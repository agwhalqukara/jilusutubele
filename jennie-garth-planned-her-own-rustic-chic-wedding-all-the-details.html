Jennie Garth Planned Her Own Rustic-Chic Wedding: All the Details

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Impressive! Jennie Garth took her rustic-chic wedding into her own hands. The Beverly Hills, 90210 alum, who looked stunning in a floral-embroidered long-sleeved gown, planned her July 11 wedding to now-husband David Abrams without the help of an event planner, sources tell Us Weekly exclusively.

“The wedding was so beautiful…she did the whole thing herself — the chandeliers and everything. She and her team put everything together,” one source tells Us. “It was obviously a lot of work but…it was very romantic,” the insider recalls, adding that everything was outside and “they had tea lights and little balls of light hanging from the trees.”

The blushing bride’s groom also took elements of the wedding into his own hands too. According to the source, Abrams said vows to all three of Garth’s daughters before exchanging them with her. “It was so sweet, one by one,” the insider tells Us. “He made everyone cry. He had different vows for each of the girls. He said: ‘I’m so honored to take on the role of your stepfather. You guys are like my daughters.’ He said he loves and respects them. It was really sweet.” Garth shares Luca, 17, Lola, 12, and Fiona, 8, with ex-husband Peter Facinelli.

Guests, including pal and former costar Tori Spelling, then enjoyed Southern food, including grits and chicken, and danced the night away while sipping on moonshine cocktails after the intimate ceremony, which took place at Garth’s Los Olivos, Calif., ranch home.
