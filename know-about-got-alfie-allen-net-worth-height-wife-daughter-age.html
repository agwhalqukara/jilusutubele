Know About GOT Alfie Allen; Net Worth, Height, Wife, Daughter, Age

Another English actor to mount fame via the massive platform of epic fantasy series Game of Thrones is 32-years-of-age Alfie Allen. His character portrayal of an arrogant, cocky and proud Theon Greyjoy yields him stupendous fanbase.

Growing up in the family related to acting is what adjured Alfie Allen to set foot in the entertainment field. He was just 12 when he announced his professional debut with the Channel 4 comedy flick, You Are Here in 1998.

Short Bio: Age, Height, Parents

Alfie Allen, age 32, came to this world as Alfie Evan Allen on 12 September 1986. He was born in Hammersmith, London, to the entertainment stars parents. Alfie’s father, Keith Allen is a Welsh-born actor and comedian while his mother, Alison Owen announces oneself as a film producer. Besides, Alfie is the younger brother of his acclaimed English singer and songwriter sister, Lily Allen. 

Standing at a tall height of 5 feet and 7 inches (1.7 m), Alfie commenced his education with Windlesham House School in Sussex. Later, he joined Embley Park School as well as St John’s College.  Subsequently, he enrolled at the Fine Arts College in Hampstead for his A-levels.

Career, Net Worth

A young and fresh start to the acting career saw Alfie Allen cast aside his sister, Lily in the 1998 British bio-drama, Elizabeth, which was their mother’s production. Afterward, he earned more experience with the movie and TV projects including Agent Cody Banks 2: Destination London (2004), Atonement (2007), Casualty 1907 (2008), and Free Fall (2009).

Coming to the seven seasons, Theon’s journey from arrogant and cocky to defenseless and regretful is what makes the character in the hype.

Also known for his roles on John Wick (2014), and The Predator (2018), Alfie will appear as John Kite in upcoming comedy film How to Build a Girl. To this point in his career, Alfie Allen enjoys a commanding net worth of $6 Million.
