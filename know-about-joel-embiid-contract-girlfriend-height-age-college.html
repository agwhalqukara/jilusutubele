Joel stands at the height of 7 feet 0 in (2.13 m) tall and weighs around 127 kg.

Career & Net Worth

The 25-year-old has a net worth of $35 million from the professional NBA since 2014. Sometime later on 20 June 2014, he had to go surgery on a broken navicular bone in his right foot. To which he was out of the league for almost six months playing for the Philadelphia 76ers. The 2016/2017 was his rookie season, and after it was about to conclude, he signed a large contract with the team.

The agreement was for a five-year, worth $148 million that included maximum deal extension. It also included an additional $30 million if he was able to secure an All-NBA first played or the 2017/2018 MVP.
