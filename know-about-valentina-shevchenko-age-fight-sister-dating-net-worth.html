Know About Valentina Shevchenko; Age, Fight, Sister, Dating, Net Worth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 4, 2024

The female flyweight champion Valentina Shevchenko is getting one after another success in recent days. She won the fight against Jessica Eye to defend her flyweight title at UFC 238 held on 9 June 2019.

Valentina Shevchenko is a Kyrgyzstani–Peruvian professional mixed martial artist who is ranked #14 in UFC pound-for-pound rankings. The former Muay Thai fighter competes in the women’s flyweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC).

Valentina Shevchenko Bio; Family, Sister, Height

Valentina was born on 7 March 1988 to Kyrgyzstani family in Frunze, Kirghizia, USSR. Her mother Elena Shevchenko raised her along with sister Antonina Shevchenko. Antonina is a Muay Thai fighter and mixed martial artist currently competing in the flyweight division of the UFC.

Valentina Shevchenko with her sister Antonina Shevchenko at the UFC238 Media day on 7 June 2019 (Picture: Antonina’s Instagram)

Shevchenko received an undergraduate degree in Film Directing from the University of Arts Kyrgyzstan. The MMA fighter stands at the height of 5 feet 5 inches (1.65 meters) and weighs 125 lbs (57 kg).

Her Career; MMA, Fight

Valentina began her fighting career in 2003, fighting in K-1, Muay Thai, and kickboxing. She won 8 gold medals competing in the IFMA world championships, as well as the IMFA Royal World Cup in 2015. She is regarded as one of the best female Muay Thai fighters in the world.

Valentina Shevchenko during a fight against Jessica Eye at UFC 238 on 9 June 2019 (Picture: MMA Fighting)

In 2014, Shevchenko signed for MMA and kickboxing. She became champion at UFC 231 beating Joanna Jędrzejczyk, whom she had previously defeated three times in amateur Muay Thai bouts. She is the current Women’s Flyweight champion as of 2019.

Valentina has summoned the remarkable net worth from her fighting career. She earns millions per year from the multi-million contract with the UFC.

Valentina Shevchenko Ranks Second in 2020 UFC Ranking!

Valentine has been able to get the second UFC spot as of 23 November 2020 citing the UFC women’s pound-for-pound rankings. During the start of her 2020 season, Valentina sealed a big win against Katlyn Chookagian on 8 February 2020. She won the fight via TKO in the third round and was able to regain her Flyweight title for the third consecutive time.

On 6 June 2020, she was scheduled to face Joanne Calderwood at UFC 250. However, a leg injury pulled her out of the fight. Before claiming the second UFC ranking, she got the fortune to defend her fourth title defenses against Jennifer Maia on 21 November 2020 at UFC 255 and won the fight via unanimous decision.

Dating Status

Despite the successful career, it looks like Valentina is yet to accomplish her dating life. The 31-year-old has not disclosed her relationship status to the media eyes.

Surfing through her Instagram, it seems like she is actively involved in her profession. She is currently enjoying her professional life rather than searching for a partner for her life. Her focus is sharp towards inching the first UFC ranking and the hype is even more from her recent big win against Jennifer Maia to secure the Flyweight title. Also, the reticent nature of Valentina on her personal life creates more speculation of her probable singleness or likely dating she must have been keeping away from the reach of the public limelight. 

Some Interesting Facts About Valentina Shevchenko

  • Her sister Antonina made her MMA debut in 2002 and returned after a 12-year break in 2017.
  • At the age of 12, she defeated a 22-year-old opponent that earned her the moniker “Bullet” from her trainer because of her speed in the ring.
  • In addition to her native Russian, she is fluent in the Spanish language.
  • She appeared in the 2020 sports film, Bruised starring Halle Berry.
  • Valentina is a vivid social media user with more than 1.6 million Instagram followers.
