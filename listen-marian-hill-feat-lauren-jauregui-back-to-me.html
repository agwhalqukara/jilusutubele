LISTEN: Marian Hill feat. Lauren Jauregui - 'Back To Me'

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, September 19, 2024

The wait is finally over! Earlier this week, Fifth Harmony’s Lauren Jauregui revealed that she was working on a track with music duo, Marian Hill. Fans were not expecting the track to come so soon after the announcement but after revealing the track title and sharing a teaser, fans were so excited. Now ‘Back To Me’ is officially out!

‘Back To Me’ was first exclusively revealed a day early by The Fader but is now available worldwide on Vevo, Spotify, and iTunes.

The track resembles the similar sounds and feel of most Marian Hill songs. It includes a heavy rhythmic beat along with electronic twists. It is the perfect sound for Lauren Jauregui to showcase her deep and sultry voice in a new way. The two voices of both Lauren and Samantha Gongol blend together beautifully to create this dynamic track that is sure to take over the charts.

“Love to watch you try and turn your back to me. But I know that any minute you’ll be back to me”

‘Back To Me’ is a fiery track about knowing someone can’t resist you and will be crawling back to you but they need to act fast and know what they want or they’ll lose you.

We know we are going to be jamming hard to ‘Back to Me’! Huge congrats to both Marian Hill and Lauren Jauregui! What do you think of ‘Back To Me’? Tweet us your thoughts at @CelebMix
