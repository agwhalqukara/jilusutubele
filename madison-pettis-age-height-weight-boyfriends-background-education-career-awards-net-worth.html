Madison’s Prosseional Career

Madison Pettis started her career as a child model when she was featured on the cover of FortWorthChild magazine after her mother entered her in a contest.

She then appeared on the children’s show Barney & Friends as Bridget in 2005 and 2006.

She got her big break when she landed the role of Peyton Kelly, the long-lost daughter of Dwayne Johnson’s character, in the Disney film The Game Plan in 2007.

Additionally, she received positive reviews for her performance and won several awards for it.

She then starred as Sophie Martinez, the president’s daughter, on the Disney Channel sitcom Cory in the House from 2007 to 2008.

Additionally, she also guest-starred on Hannah Montana as the same character in one episode.
