Michael Sutthakorn Covers 'This Is What You Came For'

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

Michael Sutthakorn has done a cover of Calvin Harris and Rihanna song ‘This Is What You Came For.’

Michael, an 19 year old singer/songwriter from Belfast, has been busy over the past few months – doing a tour in January, visiting London, Glasgow, Newcastle and Liverpool, and performing his first headline show in his home town, Belfast. Amongst all this, he also released his debut single ‘Trouble.’

Prior to his solo career, Michael was in M.A.D. which came to an end in 2014. At the start of 2015, he joined Adrenalin Trip, however, when they went their separate ways, he decided to launch a solo career.

Over the past year, he has made a good name for himself in the music industry, supporting artists such as Union J and Luke Friend and has built up a huge following on social media.
