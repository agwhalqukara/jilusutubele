Savannah Guthrie Reveals Secret to Losing Her Post-Pregnancy Baby Weight!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 18, 2024

New mom confessions!

On last night’s episode of Bravo’s ‘Watch What Happens Live!’ Savannah Guthrie admitted cutting alcohol from her diet helped her lose the baby weight after giving birth to daughter Vale last August.

“Savannah, you look amazing. I’m trying to lose those last 10 pounds of baby weight — any tips?” a viewer asked the 43-year-old ‘Today Show’ host via Facebook during the show.

Savannah with Al Roker and Andy Cohen on ‘WWHL’ on Jan. 28.

“Oh my gosh, don’t drink as much. That’s what I had to do!” Savannah replied. “Really, honestly, and actually I still have five pounds to lose, too, just to get [back] to what I weighed before.”

Related Story: Savannah Guthrie Shares New Photos of Baby Vale!

Savannah — who appeared on the nightly talk show with fellow ‘Today’ co-host Al Roker — married communications consultant Michael Feldman, 46, in March 2014 and welcomed Vale five months later.

Savannah, Mike and Vale celebrate Halloween in NYC.

“Even though being a mom is the most challenging thing I’ve ever tried to do, I’ve never felt more exhilarated and relaxed and peaceful and joyous,” she previously said of new motherhood. “Even on those nights when she’s waking up every couple of hours. I just feel thankful.”
