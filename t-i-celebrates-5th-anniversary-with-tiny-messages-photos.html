T.I. Celebrates 5th Anniversary With Tiny: Messages, Photos

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, September 13, 2024

A whole hand of happily wedded bliss! T.I. celebrated his fifth wedding anniversary with his wife, Tiny, on July 31, and the VH1 reality stars naturally exchanged lovey-dovey messages via social media this past weekend.

"5yrs ago today @majorgirl got sworn into da south's most infamous #HarrisMorganMoB," the Paper Trail hitmaker (nee Clifford Harris) wrote on Instagram, alongside a wedding photo of the couple dancing at their reception. "Our wedding was more like da Party/Rave/Night of da century….and we still #Turnt!!!!#PipedUpFaNights #ElDebargeOndaMic #WeMADEit #DaBIGnickelBag lol Love U!!! #Happy[Anniversary]MrsHarris"

The rapper also posted a separate pic from their nuptials, featuring some of their close celebrity pals like Usher, Chris Rock, and Nelly. "This pic best represents that night!!!! #S—sAblur!!!!!" he wrote. "All my Patnas Who made it dey business to share da moment wit me #PreeshateIT yall tag Em if u can see em…. #S—thatMATTERS #Happy[Anniversary]MrsHarris."

Tiny (nee Tameka Cottle) was equally ecstatic about their milestone together. "5yrs ago I walked down the isle to meet the love of my life..the man that changed my life for the better," she wrote alongside another wedding snap. "Gave me beautiful kids/family. & I'm so happy to say…That's my fine sex symbol u looking at @troubleman31 Mr. Harris, my other half,my partner. I Love You For Life. Thx to my bestie #TiffanyAlexander for having pix of this special night! ???????????? #MrAndMrsHarris #5YearsStrong #WeddingOf2010 #JamieFoxSungMeDownTheIsle #ElDebargeSungMyFirstDance #WhatANightToRemember."

The happy wife, 40, also posted a photo with her husband, 34, taking a romantic horseback ride through the woods to commemorate their special day. "Riding out on this thang," she wrote. "#MrAndMrsHarris #5thYearAnniversary."

The couple, who share four kids together and expose their lives on a VH1 reality show, T.I. & Tiny, have dealt with their fair share of issues, but seem to be in a happy place these days. "We are really good now," Tiny told Us last summer. "For us, we spend a lot of time together and we have a lot of love for each other and we … try to cater to each other in different ways."
